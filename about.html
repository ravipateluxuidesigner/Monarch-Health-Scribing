Our Story

Built by people who understand clinical work

Monarch Health Scribing was founded on a simple observation: clinicians spend too much of their day on documentation instead of patients.

We bring together certified medical scribes and secure, AI-assisted tools to take that burden off your shoulders. Every note we produce is reviewed by a trained professional and delivered straight into your EHR — accurate, compliant, and on time. From solo practices to multi-site groups, we tailor our support to the way your team already works, scaling up or down as your needs change.

Our Mission

To deliver effortless, accurate, and streamlined medical documentation — freeing clinicians to focus on patient care rather than paperwork.

Our Vision

A healthcare system where administrative load never stands between a clinician and their patient — supported by people and technology working together.

Our Values

Accuracy, confidentiality, and accountability guide every engagement. We treat your patients' data — and your trust — with the seriousness they deserve.
